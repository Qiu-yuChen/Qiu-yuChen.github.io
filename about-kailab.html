🏛️ About KAI Lab

KAI Lab (Knowledge, AI, and Innovation Lab) is led by Prof. Kai Wang at City University of Hong Kong (Dongguan). Our research focuses on computer vision, generative models, and embodied AI.

🔬 Research Areas

  • Diffusion Models — Image/video generation and editing
  • 3D Vision — Reconstruction, neural rendering, 3D generation
  • Visual Tracking — Object tracking, pose estimation
  • Embodied AI — Robot perception, AI agents, edge deployment
